Projects & Collaborations

Ensemble “Les Promenades”

The Baroque ensemble “Les Promenades” was born in 2020 in Luxembourg. It features Irina Kalderon (soprano), Laurie Krier (recorder) and Geert Hellemans (cembalo). The ensemble collaborates with many Baroque musicians in Luxembourg and across Europe. Their repertoire spans André Campra, Antoine Dard, G.Ph. Telemann, J.S. Bach, François Couperin, M.-A. Charpentier, Henry Purcell, Barbara Strozzi, Etorre Bernabei, J.Ph. Rameau, Claudio Monteverdi, Francesco Cavalli, Luigi Rossi, G.F. Händel, Pietro Torri, Alessandro Scarlatti, Bonifazio Graziani and others.

Opera Projects

Irina has performed opera roles including La fée, le Rossignol and la Bergère (L’enfant et les sortilèges), Carolina (Il matrimonio segreto), Olympia (Les Contes d’Hoffmann), Sandrina (La finta giardiniera), Pamina (Die Zauberflöte), Adina (L’elisir d’amore), Norina (Don Pasquale), Gretel (Hänsel und Gretel) and Calisto (La Calisto). In 2025 she takes part in Dido and Aeneas (Belinda) in London, L’incornazione di Poppea (Drusilla) in France and L’enfant et les sortilèges in Luxembourg.

Other Collaborations

Irina collaborates with a wide range of musicians and music companies including La Compagnie du Globe, Ensemble OrQuesta, Sequenda Opera Studio, Marcio da Silva, Dena Georgieva, Fons van der Linden, André Roe, Sophy Pathy, Sandra Silvio, Pierre Trocellier, Catherine Escure, Emmanuelle de Negri, Simona Genkova, Matthias Rajczyk, Manfred Logeling, Luisa Mauro, Jean‑Nico Schambourg, Emanuelle Bizien, Sara Carneiro, Predrag Gosta, Alessandro Urbano, Umberto Finazzi, François Joubert‑Caillet, Jean‑Claude Braun, Gabriele Natilla and others.

Irina Kalderon
Professional soprano specializing in Baroque music and contemporary classical music. Based in Luxembourg and Sofia.
